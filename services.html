A Focus on Risk Management

Ben can help identify your project’s clinical, technical and regulatory risks so they can be resolved early in the development process. Your company’s value increases as risks are mitigated.

Ben can help you reach “proof of concept”, a critical milestone for fundraising in record time. The prototypes may not be pretty, but they will answer the right questions and inspire confidence in your design direction.

Ben can help design and execute a product development program based on continued risk management and contingency planning that is compliant with the FDA-QSR. This ensures you get the right product, for the right market, at the right price.

Working with Clawson Device Group

Clawson Device Group’s product development laboratory and offices are located in Santa Cruz, California. Ben supervises a broad network of engineers and suppliers who perform product development tasks both on location and remotely. This staffing model delivers maximum capital efficiency to Clawson Device Group clients.

While every relationship is different, Ben engages most clients on one of three levels:

Startup coaching – Council, analysis and feedback to assist with fundraising presentations, regulatory strategy, networking and technology issues.

Program Manager – Supervises research and development of your medical device. Responsibilities include recruitment and management of appropriate contributors and suppliers, execution of design and design verification, and delivery of a product to an established specification.

Consulting Executive – In some cases Ben may function as a consulting CEO, CTO or COO, depending on the availability and occupation of your principals.
