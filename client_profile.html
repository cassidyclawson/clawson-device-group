CLIENT PROFILE

With so many competent service providers and contract manufacturers in the medical device sector, many entrepreneurs and startups are launching new medical devices with little or no investment in offices, permanent employees or capital equipment. Clawson Device Group helps facilitate this type of minimalist corporate structure by providing comprehensive and efficient management and product development services.

Clawson Device Group works with medical device startups of various size in a variety medical markets.

The funded or unfunded entrepreneur/investor who wants to prepare his or her product for an acquisition. These clients try to maximize their exit with the least investment.

The startup founded by a group of physicians, engineers and/or MBAs. These clients often pursue venture/angel funding with the intention of bringing their product to market.

The established medical device company that wants to solve some persistent technical problem or develop new products with greater capital efficiency.
